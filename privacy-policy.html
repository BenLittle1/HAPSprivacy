Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Haps ("the app") is built with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

None. Haps does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Location Data

Haps requests access to your device's location to provide turn-by-turn navigation. Your location data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Used only to determine your position on the map and provide navigation directions
  • Never sent to any server, third party, or external service
  • Never stored or logged beyond the current navigation session

Haps may request background location access so haptic navigation can continue while your phone is in your pocket. Background location data is handled identically — on-device only, never transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Haps uses Apple MapKit for map display and route calculation. Map tile requests and directions queries are handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. Haps does not add any additional tracking or data collection on top of these Apple services.

Children's Privacy

Haps does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
